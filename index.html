Types of Cyber Insurance Coverage

At SD Group, we offer a range of cyber insurance coverage options to ensure comprehensive protection:

  • Cyber Liability Insurance: Protects against legal liabilities arising from cyber incidents.
  • Business Interruption Insurance: Covers loss of income due to cyber attacks.
  • Cyber Extortion Insurance: Provides coverage for ransom payments and related expenses.
